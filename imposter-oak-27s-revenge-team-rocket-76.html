Imposter Oak's Revenge
にせオーキドの逆襲 False Okido's Counterattack

Imposter Oak's Revenge (Japanese: にせオーキドの逆襲 False Okido's Counterattack) is a Trainer card. It is part of the Team Rocket expansion.

Card text

Discard a card from your hand in order to play this card. Your opponent shuffles his or her hand into his or her deck, then draws 4 cards.

Gallery

Trivia

The name printed on the card is printed "Imposter Oak's Revenge". The word is spelled differently on this card than it was on the later Impostor Professor Oak's Invention card and some early prints of the earlier Imposter Professor Oak card, both of which also featured the same character.
