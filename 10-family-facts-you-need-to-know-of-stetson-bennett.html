10 Family Facts You Need to Know Of Stetson Bennett

1.  Who is Stetson Bennett

Stetson Fleming Bennett IV, a quarterback for the Georgia Bulldogs in American football, was born on October 28, 1997. He began his career as a walk-on at Georgia before transferring to Jones County Junior College and earning the moniker “The Mailman.” In 2022 and 2023, Bennett returned to Georgia and won back-to-back national titles.

9. His family moves to georgia

On October 28, 1997, Bennett was born in Atlanta, Georgia. His family relocated to Nahunta, Georgia, from Atlanta in the first grade. Bennett’s family relocated to Blackshear, Georgia when he was in the eighth grade, where he attended Pierce County High School and played high school football.
