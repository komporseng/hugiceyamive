Release Date, Recap, and Where to Read

Publish date: 2024-05-03

Get ready for the highly anticipated return of the action-packed comedy series “Return of the Mount Hua Sect” as fans eagerly await Chapter 79. The release date is now revealed, and excitement is in the air. In the midst of a fierce battle between the Mount Hua Sect and the Wudang Sect, the previous chapter left readers on the edge of their seats. Baek Chun takes charge, leading the fight against the formidable main disciple of the Wudang Sect, while Chung Myung gleefully revels in the chaos.

Produced by Studio Lico, known for their exceptional work, this manhwa is set to once again captivate audiences with its blend of comedy and ruthless fight scenes. Fans have been eagerly awaiting this announcement to delve into the world of Chung Myung and experience the thrilling humor and intense action that awaits them.

Return of the Mount Hua Sect Chapter 79: Release Date

Prepare for the epic return of “Return of the Mount Hua Sect” with Chapter 79, set to unleash its mind-blowing action on July 4, 2023, at 8:30 pm IST! Brace yourself for an unforgettable experience!

Chapter 78 Recap

In the previous chapter, the senior brother of the Wudang Sect launches an attack on the Hua Shadow Sect, mocking their strength. Jong Yoon and Jo Gul notice the resemblance between the senior brother’s demeanor and Baek Chun’s past behavior, hinting at a connection. Meanwhile, a disciple from the Wudang Sect reveals that their Plum Blossom Supreme was defeated by the Taiji sword emperor of their sect. Chung Myung recalls his previous life and the humiliation he inflicted upon the Taiji Sword Emperor.

With a single swing of his sword, Chung Myung demolishes a building, expressing his love for fighting in front of an audience. He assigns Baek Chun the task of confronting the main disciple of the Wudang Sect, while issuing orders to the rest of the group. The battle commences, and both Jong Yoon and Jo Gul easily defeat their opponents. Baek Chun engages in a fierce confrontation with the main disciple. The chapter concludes at this intense moment, leaving readers eager to discover the outcome of their clash.

Where to Read?

The highly anticipated Chapter 79 of “Return of the Mount Hua Sect” can be exclusively enjoyed on Naver, where the official raw version will be available. For the official English translation, fans can dive into the thrilling story on the Webtoon app. Don’t miss out on the chance to experience this captivating chapter in both its original and translated versions.
