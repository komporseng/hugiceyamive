Iyanna Mayweather

Publish date: 2024-09-08

Iyanna Mayweather is the daughter of former pound-for-pound boxing king Floyd Mayweather. She is an Instagram star with more than 1.2 million followers.

Bio & Family

Iyanna Mayweather was born on May 20, 2000, in Nevada, the United States. She is the daughter of Floyd Mayweather and Melissa Brim. Her mother is a beautician and entrepreneur.

Iyanna Mayweather pictured with her father Floyd Mayweather (EssentiallySports)

She has three half-siblings; two brothers Koraun and Zion and a sister named Jirah Mayweather. Her birth sign is Taurus.

More: Jessica Delp

Career & Net Worth

Iyanna is now not involved in any profession. However, she came to the spotlight in April 2020 after being arrested. She allegedly stabbed Lapattra Lashai Jacobs, NBA Youngboy’s baby mama. She was later released from jail on $30,000 bond.

As a daughter of prominent boxer, she enjoys the lavish lifestyle. Meanwhile, her father has a net worth of $505 million.

Personal Life

The 19-year-old Iyanna keeps her personal life confined. However, she was in a relationship with her rapper boyfriend NBA Youngboy. 

She even allegedly stabbed the baby mama of her Lapattra Lashai Jacobs in April 2020.
