Biography

Pat Cleveland was born in New York City, NY on June 23, 1950. Fashion model who rose to stardom in the 70s for becoming one of the first African-Americans to achieve great success as a print and fashion model. She worked with some of the biggest names in the industry, including Diana Vreeland, Yves Saint Laurent, and Oscar de la Renta. She served as a muse for painter Salvador Dali. She graduated from Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School and studied design at New York’s High School of Art and Design.
