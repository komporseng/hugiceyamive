Why Molly Qerim is a Must Know name in Sports Media

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Molly Qerim is an American television personality and host of ESPN's First Take. Her impactful presence and exceptional skills in sports media have solidified her as a prominent figure in the industry. 

Early Career and Professional Journey

Molly Qerim began her career in sports media at ESPN in 2006, where she contributed to digital and mobile platforms, earning an Emmy in 2008 for her work on Fantasy Football Now.

Her early experiences at ESPN involved conducting interviews with athletes and celebrities, covering major sporting events such as the Super Bowl, the Heisman Trophy presentation, the NBA Draft, the NBA All-Star Game, and the MLB All-Star game, providing on-site reporting and interviews.

Qerim's versatility and expertise enabled her to thrive in front of a live audience, particularly when First Take traveled to marquee sporting events, showcasing her ability to connect with a wide variety of guests through interview segments.

Hosting Skills and Notable Shows

Qerim's hosting skills have been evident throughout her career. She has hosted or co-hosted various shows across different networks, including serving as the original host of MMA Live when it debuted on ESPN.com.

Additionally, she was an anchor and reporter for the NFL Network, hosting NFL AM and NFL Fantasy Live from 2012 to 2015. Her proficiency as a host led her to be named the host of First Take in 2015, a position she continues to hold with both warmth and authority, working alongside prominent a voice in the industry, Stephen A. Smith.

Impact and Coverage of Major Sporting Events

Qerim's impact on the industry is further evidenced by her extensive coverage of major sporting events.

She has covered multiple Super Bowls, the Heisman Trophy presentation, the NBA draft, the NBA All-Star Game, and the MLB All-Star game, providing on-site reporting and interviews.

Her breadth of experience with celebrity red carpets, A-list musical and TV/film guest interviews, UFC/MMA coverage, tennis, boxing, and more, demonstrates her versatility and wide-ranging expertise in sports media.

Recognitions and Philanthropy

Throughout her career, Qerim has received several accolades, including an Emmy for her contribution to Fantasy Football Now and being inducted into the Quinnipiac University Hall of Fame class in 2020.

Off camera, she is a dedicated philanthropist and ambassador for organizations such as the Boys and Girls Club of America and the international justice mission. She is also an advocate for endometriosis awareness, using her platform to bring attention to important health issues.

Molly Qerim's journey in sports media has been marked by her exceptional hosting skills, extensive coverage of major sporting events, and impactful philanthropic endeavors.

Her career trajectory, from her early days at ESPN to her current role as the host of First Take on ESPN, showcases her unwavering dedication and influence in the industry.

Through her notable interviews, hosting prowess, and commitment to raising awareness for important causes, Molly Qerim has undeniably solidified her position as a must-know name in sports media.

Molly Qerim's multifaceted contributions to the field of sports media have left an indelible mark, and her impact continues to resonate across the industry.
