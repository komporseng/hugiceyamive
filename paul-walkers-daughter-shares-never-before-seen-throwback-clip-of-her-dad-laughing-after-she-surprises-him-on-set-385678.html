Paul Walkers Daughter Shares Never-Before-Seen Throwback Clip Of Her Dad Laughing After She Surpr

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Paul Walker’s daughter, Meadow, took to her social media to share a never before seen throwback clip of her late actor father laughing, reminding people just what a bright person he was. The throwback video was taken on set when the daughter visited him as a surprise.

The post was also a big surprise for Walker’s fans as it comes more than six years since his untimely death.

Now, Meadow is 21 years old and it seems like she misses her dad a lot.

The video was apparently taken by her at the time and showed Paul receiving a scare from her at first since she just showed up on set unannounced!

However, his next instinctual reaction was to just burst out laughing.

It’s a bittersweet reminder that the actor used to be a very happy and energetic person who would just light up any room and cheer anyone up.

‘What the hell? You just scared the hell out of me! What the hell are you doing? No way…no way!’ the excited dad gushed, obviously very happy to see his child.

Then Meadow rushes into his arms and they have the sweetest hug.

As you probably remember, Paul Walker lost his life when the Porsche he was a passenger in crashed into a tree during a fundraiser car show.

The tragedy happened on November 30, 2013, shocking many.

In the caption, the daughter wrote: ‘I never thought I’d share this. But it felt right. Be good. I love you. Stay safe. Xx.’

She was, of course, referring to the current coronavirus, COVID-19 pandemic that has been affecting the whole world.
