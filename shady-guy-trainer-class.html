From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Shady Guy (Japanese: 怪しい男 Shady Guy) is a type of Pokémon Trainer that first debuted in the Generation III games, appearing in Pokémon Colosseum. It refers to two Trainers, temporarily. The first is Folly, who is later revealed to be a Miror B.Peon. The second is Fein who masquerades as Wes. During the battle, he is called Shady Guy Wes. He is later revealed to be a Cipher Peon.
