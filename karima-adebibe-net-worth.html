Karima Adebibe Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-06-25

Karima Adebibe has accumulated a net worth of $2 million. She is known for her work as a Moroccan and English actress as well as a fashion model. She has been involved in various movies and TV series such as “Fearless,” “Vikings,” “Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows,” and “AVP: Alien vs. Predator.”
