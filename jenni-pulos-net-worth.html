Jenni Pulos Nassos Wiki Biography

Born on January 3, 1973, in Portland, Oregon USA, Jenni Pulos is of Greek and American ancestry. She is a television personality and a hip hop artist. Pulos is best known for her role as the assistant to television star and real estate investor Jeff Lewis, with whom she has appeared on the reality television series “Flipping Out” (2007 – present) and “Interior Therapy with Jeff Lewis” (2012 – present). She has been an active figure in the entertainment industry since 2007.

According to authoritative sources, Pulos’ wealth is estimated to be $2.5 million as of mid-2016, with television, music, and business endorsements being her primary sources of income.

Jenni Pulos was raised in Portland, where she initially pursued a career in tennis before changing her focus to acting and majoring in Theatre and Film at the University of California.

Pulos’ career has been closely associated with Jeff Lewis, and she debuted in the main cast of “Flipping Out”. The series was nominated for an Emmy Award. Additionally, the spinoff show “Interior Therapy with Jeff Lewis” has significantly contributed to Pulos’ net worth.

Pulos also works as a writer, actor, and producer, involved in developing other reality shows and making guest appearances on various programs. She has also ventured into music, releasing a studio album for children titled “Old School Kids Beats” (2009) and winning an Apollo Award for her hip hop performances.

In 2014, Pulos published a novel titled “Grin and Bear It: How to Be Happy No Matter What Reality Throws Your Way”, which received positive reviews from critics.

In her personal life, Pulos was previously married to Chris Elwood, before divorcing in 2008. She later married orthopaedic surgeon Dr. Jonathan Nassos, with whom she has three children. The family divides their time between residences in Los Angeles and New York City.
