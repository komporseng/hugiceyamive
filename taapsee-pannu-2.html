Taapsee Pannu Wiki/Biography

Born on 01st August 1987, Taapsee Pannu’s age is 35 Years as of 2021. She was born in a middle-class family from Delhi, India. She was born and brought up in New Delhi, India.

She completed her schooling from Mata Jai Kaur Public School, Ashok Vihar, Delhi, and later went on to pursue her graduation in the field of Computer science engineering from Guru Tegh Bahadur Institute of Technology, New Delhi. She started working at the age of 18 as a model while she was still in college. After completing her graduation Taapsee worked as a professional computer engineer before becoming a model.

It is not known if she has taken any formal training to polish her acting skills after completing her graduation. Taapsee stays in Mumbai and has a wedding planning company in collaboration with her sister and a friend.
