Publish date: 2024-05-19

It’s been six years since ‘Stranger Things’ made its premiere on Netflix and took the world by storm. Since then, the show has turned out to become one of the most popular shows in the world and has broken several records. Season 4 of the sci-fi series just made its way to the streaming platform and earned rave reviews. It also broke the viewing records and became the second most-watched Netflix series of all time.

The “super-sized” edition featured nine episodes and the average duration for each episode was 1 hour and 15 minutes, with the finale being over 2 hours and 30 minutes. Critics, as well as audiences, have praised the actors for their impeccable performances, especially Sadie Sink and Joseph Quinn. Apart from receiving praise for their acting, the stars of the show have been receiving a handsome amount of money for being a part of the show.

Most of the actors on the show gained worldwide recognition with their respective roles in the series and laid a strong foundation for their acting careers. But not a lot of people know about the fortune they have made through the show and their other acting projects. Let’s dig deeper and get to know details surrounding their finances.

Winona Ryder - $18 million

The most renowned actor on the Netflix show and probably, the most celebrated one, Winona Ryder is undoubtedly one of the most interesting aspects of ‘Stranger Things’. The actress made his feature film debut in 1986 with ‘Lucas’. However, she garnered wider recognition with Tim Burton’s ‘Beetlejuice’. She garnered critical acclaim and two consecutive Academy Award nominations for ‘The Age of Innocence’ (1993) and ‘Little Women’ (1994).

She has been a part of ‘Stranger Things’ since 2016 and plays the role of Joyce Byers. According to Celebrity Net Worth, she has a net worth of $18 million.

Millie Bobby Brown - $10 million

One of the brightest young talents working in Hollywood right now, Millie Bobby Brown took the world by storm with ‘Stranger Things’. Her riveting portrayal of Eleven laid a strong foundation for her acting career and gave her amazing opportunities. She earned a whopping $1 million for her debut movie ‘Godzilla: King of Monsters’. The actress was also seen in the Netflix movie ‘Enola Holmes’ alongside Henry Cavill and made a staggering $6.1 million from the movie.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Millie has around $10 million to her name, which is very impressive going by her age.

David Harbour - $6 million

Another actor who gained worldwide recognition because of the Netflix series and became an instant superstar. David Harbour is perhaps one of the most-loved characters on the show and as far as his acting career is concerned, the star made his TV debut in 1999 with ‘Law & Order’. Meanwhile, in 2004, he made his feature film debut. Harbour was seen in a supporting role in the hit 2005 movie ‘Brokeback Mountain’. The actor received a lot of praise for her acting in ‘Law & Order: Criminal Intent’ in which he played the role of a serial killer named Paul Devildis.

Apart from that, he has been featured in projects such as ‘The Newsroom’, ‘Black Mass’, ‘Suicide Squad’, ‘Hellboy’, and ‘Banshee’. As per Celebrity Net Worth, Harbour’s worth is estimated at $6 million. 

Gaten Matarazzo - $5 million

Matarazzo’s first acting gig was in the Broadway musical ‘Priscilla, Queen of the Desert’ and also joined the ‘Les Miserables’ in 2013. On the other hand, his TV debut came in the year 2015 with ‘The Blacklist’ and was subsequently cast as Dustin in ‘Stranger Things’. The actor has been nominated for three Screen Actors Guild awards for his role of Dustin Henderson in ‘Stranger Things’.

Celebrity Net Worth reveals that Henderson’s worth is estimated at $5 million.

Finn Wolfhard - $4 million

The young actor, who plays Mike Wheeler on ‘Stranger Things’, got his first acting in 2014 with the TV series ‘100’ and went on to play the role of Jordie Pinsky in the blockbuster series ‘Supernatural’. 2016 and 2017 turned out to be his best years because he made his Netflix debut with ‘Stranger Things’ in 2016 while his movie ‘It’ turned out to be a gigantic global hit.

‘The Goldfinch’, ‘The Turning’, ‘Ghostbusters: Afterlife’, and ‘Duncanville’ are some of the projects that he has been a part of. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the actor has a net worth of roughly $4 million.

Joe Keery - $4 million

From being one of the most hated characters to one of the most adored, Joe Keery’s Steve Harrington has come a long way on ‘Stranger Things’. Before appearing on the Netflix series, the actor was seen in multiple ads for KFC, Domino’s, and Amiibo. In 2015, he made his debut in films as well as on Television. He garnered universal fame with ‘Stranger Things’ and was later seen in the movie ‘Molly’s Game’ alongside Jessica Chastain.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, he is worth around $4 million.

Natalia Dyer - $4 million

Dyer made her debut with ‘Hannah Montana: The Movie’, but her role as Nancy Wheeler in ‘Stranger Things’ made her a superstar. The actress starred in independent films, most notably ‘Yes, God, Yes’ (2019) in which she stars as Alice, a Catholic school girl exploring sexuality. In the same year, she was seen playing the role of Coco in the movie ‘Velvet Buzzsaw’ along with Jake Gyllenhaal.

Celebrity Net Worth reports that she also has a net worth of $4 million. 

Charlie Heaton - $4 million

Heaton began his professional career as a musician and played for several bands before making his acting debut. The actor was seen playing supporting roles in movies such as ‘As You Are’ and ‘Shut In’ before appearing in ‘Stranger Things’. Heaton plays the role of Jonathan Byers in the Netflix series and is Nancy Wheeler’s boyfriend.

In real life, Heaton is actually dating Natalia Dyer, and are together since 2016.

As per Celebrity Net Worth, the actor’s worth is estimated at $4 million.

Caleb McLaughlin - $3 million

Just like all the young stars, he began his acting career with the opera ‘Lost in the Stars’ and went on to make his TV debut with ‘Law & Order: Special Victims Unit’. Over the years, McLaughlin has been seen in several TV projects such as ‘What Would You Do?’, ‘Shades of Blue’, ‘Blue Bloods’, and ‘Final Space’.

However, the actor garnered worldwide recognition with ‘Stranger Things’ as Lucas Sinclair. He won the NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Performance by a Youth for the Netflix series.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the actor’s net worth is estimated at $3 million.

Noah Schnapp - $3 million

Schnapp made his acting debut with the Academy Award-winning movie ‘Bridge of Spies’ alongside Tom Hanks and Mark Rylance. Meanwhile, in 2016, the young actor made his debut on Television with ‘Stranger Things’. He plays the role of Will Byers in the series and was nominated for three SAG Awards for his role.

Celebrity Net Worth reports that Schnapp’s earnings have been estimated at $3 million.

Maya Hawke - $3 million

Following the footsteps of her parents Uma Thurman and Ethan Hawke, the young actress made her acting debut in the field of television with the TV series ‘Little Women’. On the other hand, in 2018, she made her feature film debut with ‘Ladyworld’. In 2019, she gained worldwide recognition with the Netflix show ‘Stranger Things’ and was last seen in the movie ‘Fear Street Part One: 1994’.

She has a net worth of roughly $3 million, per Celebrity Net Worth.
