Jill Latiano is an American actress, model, dancer, and television personality. Jill Latiano is best known for her roles in movies like The Devil We Know (2018), GMO OMG (2013), and Casual (2015). Apart from being a talented entertainer, she is also recognized as the wife of the popular actor cum screenwriter Glenn Howerton.

Jill Latiano – Professional Life

Latiano who now is a celebrated actress started her career as a dancer for New York Knicks in 1999. 4 years as a dancer then she appeared as a TV host of the series ‘Fashion in Focus’ for NYC Media.

During her years of hosting (2003-2005), she made her acting debut appearing as a guest spot on the TV series ‘Sex and the City’. She followed up with guest appearances in  ‘Rescue Me’ ‘Out of Practice’, ‘Heist’, ‘CSI: NY’ and ‘Ugly Betty’. 

In 2007, ‘The Virgin of Akron‘ gave her, her first lead role. She then even joined the film industry via movies like Epic Movie (2007), Lower Learning (2008), Fired Up (2009), and the psychological thriller Kalamity (2010).

Throwing a brilliant performance as Nurse Gretchen in the comedy movie “Lower Learning”, Jill earned wide popularity and success in 2008. Furthermore, she has also starred in movies like Drake and Josh, Community, Epic Movie, and much more.

Moreover, in 2011, she made an appearance in  The Chicago Code as Natalie in the Episode: “O’Leary’s Cow”. Likewise, she played the role of Katie Knox in Legit, (2014) and (2015) Casual as Amy.
