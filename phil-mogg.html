Biography

Phil Mogg was born in England on April 15, 1948. Vocalist best known as the frontman for the English metal band UFO. With the band, he has recorded more than two dozen albums, including 1974’s influential Phenomenon. He has played with guitarist Michael Schenker as a member of UFO. He founded UFO with Mick Bolton, Pete Way and Andy Parker in August 1969.
