Anna Lore top movies, TV shows and awards

Publish date: 2024-05-05

The Peacock movie “They/Them,” in which Anna Lore (born on March 15, 1993) starred as Kim opposite Kevin Bacon, Anna Chlumsky, Theo Germaine, Quei Tann, and Austin Crute, brought her international prominence at the age of 29.

Before becoming well-known for her work on the LGBTQIA+ inspiring movie “They/Them,” Lore made news for her sensational portrayal of Carrie in the “All American” series from 2020 to 2022. During that time, she also appeared in “Into the Dark” and “Doom Patrol.”

On the one hand, since she started working with Director John Logan in 2022, Lore has been floating in the spotlight, but her success will continue into the following year since she is presently developing “Gotham Knights,” in which she will debut as “Stephanie Brown.”
